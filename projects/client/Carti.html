Why Does Everybody Dresses Like Playboi Carti Now

Everybody dresses like Playboi Carti now because his style matches the emotional temperature of the moment. Clean, minimal outfits started to feel stale and corporate, while Carti’s dark, punk-inspired look feels raw and alive. Leather pants, boots, chains, and distressed clothes aren’t about looking polished — they’re about expressing chaos, emotion, and rebellion without explaining yourself.

Carti’s influence spread fast through TikTok and his Opium circle, turning a personal aesthetic into something repeatable. Once people saw the fits at concerts, in edits, and on their feeds, the look became a visual language anyone could speak. You don’t have to be Carti to dress like him — you just need the attitude and a willingness to stand out.

More than anything, Carti rewrote how masculinity looks in fashion. Makeup, tight silhouettes, jewelry, and gothic elements stopped feeling risky and started feeling natural. Dressing like Playboi Carti now isn’t about copying one rapper — it’s about rejecting polish, embracing emotion, and wearing exactly how you feel.
